PARIS

Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light" and famous for its art, fashion, and culture. Iconic landmarks include the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. It's a global hub for romance, cuisine, and timeless architecture.

TOKYO

Tokyo, the capital of Japan, blends futuristic innovation with deep-rooted tradition. From high-tech skyscrapers and anime culture to ancient temples and cherry blossoms, it's a dynamic city offering nonstop energy, culinary excellence, and unique experiences.

DUBAI

Dubai, in the United Arab Emirates, is known for its luxury lifestyle, ultra-modern architecture, and ambitious projects like the Burj Khalifa and Palm Jumeirah. It's a desert metropolis that mixes global business, tourism, and cultural diversity with Arabian heritage.
